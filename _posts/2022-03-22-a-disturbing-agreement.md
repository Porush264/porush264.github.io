He walks upstate\
Proud and unafraid\
Unaware of the deal\
That has been made \
To put him in his grave

It all started when the man \
Visited the orphan's home\
To make a donation for the children\
Who were left there alone without any second thought\
But when accidentally he had stepped on\
The egoistic child's toys\
In the eyes of that little girl\
He was now sworn enemy till the end of time

And so the child hired a woman to do the crime\
Who was well known for living this type of life\
She had experience which could speak for her\
As she was famously called by the name " Danger "

The stars above me ,signal me \
To a treasure hidden under the sea\
Where no man or woman could reach\
And corrupt themselves with thoughts of greed

This treasure, once valued by gods\
Was now lost in the unknown \
With only stars to guide through the dangerous path\
And reclaim again what was once ours

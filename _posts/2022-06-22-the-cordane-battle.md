# 3.The One Remaining

He was almost there ,the place where he had spent his childhood and now where he would have to mourn for his brother's death. He still could not believe it ,his brother -the one who they said could escape death even if it was staring him face on .Perhaps this was all a way of Hoshakh's to call him back ,because he would have known, any other way would not work in the circumstances. The cart suddenly came to a stop ,it seems they must have reached their destination, the kingdom of Orkhan ,magestic and mysterious with its big palace and their uncountable streets .Cancut looked out of the drapes ,there seemed to be a number of soldiers with their own carts standing,some on his side and some facing him
